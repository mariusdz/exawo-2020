The Beginning

Hi, this is Marius Dzikas. I am new at blogging, so I beg your pardon if something will be.. Anyway, I am here to share the stuff that i know.

Most of my life I was in close relationship with sports. I was playing and competing in football, basketball, track and field, karate, training other people.

I am 40 years of age, with some health problems(spine, joints). Recently I have been diagnosed with Meniere's disease and later coronavirus. After 20 week treatment and then 7 week self isolation it has gone away... But i gained a lot of weight (not muscle) !

So I've decided to share with you some of my training routines. To see how body changes, I will publish photos of myself every week.
Most of us have seen or heard about magic diets and super exercise sets that transform the body in no time. On this page, you will see real change of the body.

The workouts won't be difficult. Most of the time I will be exercising outside in the beautiful Finnish nature. The training sessions will contain basic exercises: push-ups, pull-ups, sit-ups, squats, hitting bag, morning exercise for deeper muscles. If you decide to start exercising, don't go too hard or rush, it could lead to overtraining and quitting. Train smart! And be consistent- this is the key to success! If you will have any questions feel free to ask.

The time limit I have set is 90 days. After that we will be in better shape. We will be faster, stronger and of course- leaner!

That's it for now, guys. Let's get ready for the battle with ourselves!

Workout

As I have planned the first week of training was not too intensive. I wanted to get back on track as smoothly as it is possible without losing motivation or getting injured. Every day I covered 5-7 kilometers while walking. Every second day exercised outside. The workouts length were 30-40 minutes. It contained light free weights with 10-12 repetitions, punching and kicking bag(self-made from used tires), squats, sit-ups, push-ups, and light stretch-up.

Workout

Monday

  • Warm up 10 min
  • Bench press 4 x 12

  • Dumbbell incline bench flys 4 x 12
  • Push ups hands narrow 4 x 10
  • Bag punch 8 x 2min
  • Shield side kicks 50 each leg

Wednesday

  • Warm up 10 min
  • Dumbbell overhead press 4 x 12
  • Dumbbell lateral raise 4 x 12
  • Dumbbell bicep curl 4 x 12
  • Dumbbell tricep curl
  • Bag punch 8 x 2min
  • Shield side kicks 50 each leg

Friday

  • Warm up 10 min
  • Barbell squats 4 x 12
  • Squats(body weight) slow motion 4 x 12
  • Russian squats (option 1) 4 x 8
  • Russian squats (option 2) 4 x 6
  • Machine upper back row 4 x 12
  • Front head raises(lying on the bench) untill its too heavy to lift
  • Side to side head raises(lying on the bench) untill its too heavy to lift
