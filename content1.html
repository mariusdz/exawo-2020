About Work'n'Train

60 minutes daily is what it takes to make your life better and feel healthier. Just 3600 seconds...
This exercise system is for working people with less physical activity at their job. Working together with physiotherapists, sports medics and coaches, we have created this training program. It prevents from shortening of muscles, makes spine, joints and tendons healthier, increases lung capacity, strengthens heart muscle, improves your posture.
Everybody has heard about benefits of exercise, but not everyone cares about their own body!
During the session we perform exercises of general fitness, muscle stretching, special breathing exercises, meditate.
