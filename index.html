FightFit

This program is for everyone who likes to punch bag and kick trainer. Sessions are based on fighter's (striker's) routine. FightFit training is typically high intensity and usually lasts for at least an hour in most cases, it burns a maximum number of calories per workout and is therefore great for anyone who wants to lose weight and lose it fast. Improving the strength, size and shape of your muscles requires you to regularly subject them to some form of resistance training. FightFit helps provide this training by requiring you to perform exercises like push-ups and squats using your own body weight.
Speed, power, stamina, reaction, balance. These are the attributes of a fighter.
